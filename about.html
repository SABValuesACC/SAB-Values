📌 About SAB Values

Our story, mission and what we’re building

🧠 Our Origin

SAB Values started as a simple idea: organize Brainrot trade values in a clean, readable way. At first, everything was messy, split across chats, guesses, and random lists made by players. This site was created to bring order to that chaos and make trading easier for everyone.

📊 What We Do

We track Brainrot values, demand, rarity, and trade trends across the community. Items are grouped by tiers like Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, Mythic, Secret, Brainrot God and OG. The goal is not just listing values, but helping players understand the market.

⚙️ Features

• Organized value lists by rarity
• Sorting by value system
• Visual tier-based pages
• High-tier highlight system (OG / Secret / Events)
• Constant updates based on community trade behavior

🔥 Philosophy

We don’t believe in fake hype or random inflation. Values are based on real trade behavior, demand, and how the community actually treats each item. Everything here is built to reflect reality, not bias.

🙏 Thanks

Thanks to everyone who uses the site, suggests updates, or helps improve the value system. This project grows with the community, not just behind it. <3
